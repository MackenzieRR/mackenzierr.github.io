MJARR

Mackenzie J.A. Reid Rostad (b. 1995) is a Canadian documentary filmmaker with a practice of participatory and lighthearted explorations of the abstractions that comprise everyday life. He is a co-creator of the counter film festival Barlinale in Berlin, Germany and an active member of the Cinema Nova collective in Brussels, Belgium where he is curently based.
