Dear Kristina

3 - 40min., 2020 - 2021, 2K/24p, Color, HD/Foundfootage, United Kingdom (EN)

A series of short experimental correspondence films between OK Pedersen and Mackenzie Reid Rostad during the years 2020-202. Writing to each other from across the Atlantic, the filmmakers explore their individual relationships with technology, media, migration, the future, and collective dreaming. Their exchange culminated in two feature films, Kingdom, Clouds, Sirens, Pirates, and Dream Sheets and Cloud Gate 2. The serries includes: WALL (5min); PAYWALL (4min); Poundland (3min); SKY (4min); Grenfell (8min); and Notting Hill (14min).
